TDS Receivable

Why This Error Occurred

TDS Receivable represents tax deducted by the customer on your behalf — money the government owes you. It is a current asset. Placing it under "Duties & Taxes" (a liability group) causes P&L and Balance Sheet distortion.

Rule violated: AS-2 / Schedule III — Receivables must be classified under Current Assets, not Liabilities.
Current Group
Duties & Taxes
Problem

Should Be Under
Current Assets

Auto-Correction — Suggested Journal Entry

Dr TDS Receivable (Current Assets) ₹10,000
Cr TDS Receivable (Duties & Taxes) — old ₹10,000

Note: No journal entry needed — just re-group the ledger in Tally. The balance will move automatically.

How to Fix in Tally

Gateway of Tally → Accounts Info → Ledgers → Alter → Select "TDS Receivable" → Change Group to Current Assets → Accept

Bank Interest Received

Why This Error Occurred

Bank Interest Received is income earned from your bank — it increases profit. Placing it under "Indirect Expenses" inflates your expenses and understates profit, leading to incorrect P&L and wrong tax computation.

Rule violated: Matching Principle — Income must be classified under Income groups, never Expense groups.
Current Group
Indirect Expenses
Problem
Income placed in Expense group
Should Be Under
Indirect Incomes

Auto-Correction — Suggested Journal Entry

Dr Bank Interest Received (Indirect Expenses) — reversal ₹25,000
Cr Bank Interest Received (Indirect Incomes) ₹25,000

Note: Re-group the ledger in Tally. If vouchers are already posted, the P&L will auto-correct after re-grouping.

How to Fix in Tally

Gateway of Tally → Accounts Info → Ledgers → Alter → Select "Bank Interest Received" → Change Group to Indirect Incomes → Accept

Difference in Opening Balance

Why This Error Occurred

A ₹15,000 debit difference in opening balances means Tally's books did not tally (Dr ≠ Cr) at the start of this financial year. This typically happens when opening balances are entered manually without balancing both sides.

Rule violated: Double Entry System — Every debit must have an equal and opposite credit. Opening balances must sum to zero.
Current Balance
Dr ₹15,000
Problem
Opening balances not matching
Should Be
Zero (₹0)

Auto-Correction — Suggested Journal Entry

Dr Suspense Account ₹15,000
Cr Difference in Opening Balance ₹15,000

Temporary fix: Park in Suspense Account while you investigate the source. Then identify the correct ledger and pass the rectification entry.

How to Fix in Tally

Gateway of Tally → Accounts Info → Ledgers → Alter → Check all opening balances → Correct Debit/Credit sides → Ensure total Dr = total Cr
