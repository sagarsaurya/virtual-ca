## What Each Plan Includes in Detail

### Free — ₹0
- 2 uploads per month
- Basic error detection (Critical only)
- No AI chat
- No compliance calendar
- No export to Tally

### Starter — ₹499/month
- 10 uploads per month
- Full error detection (Critical + Review)
- AI chat (50 messages/month)
- Compliance calendar with alerts
- Download PDF report
- 1 user

### Pro — ₹1,499/month
- Unlimited uploads
- Unlimited AI chat
- Full compliance suite (TDS, PT, GST)
- Bank reconciliation module
- Export to Tally (XML/Excel/CSV)
- Ledger drill-down
- Fix workflow (status/assign/comment)
- Up to 3 users

### CA Firm — ₹3,999/month
- Everything in Pro
- Multiple client companies
- Team access (unlimited users)
- Client-wise reports
- Assign errors to team members
- Priority support
- White-label reports (coming Phase 3)
