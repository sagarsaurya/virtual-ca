## Balance Sheet Audit Checklist

### Fixed Assets
- [ ] Fixed asset register maintained with: date of purchase, cost, depreciation rate, WDV
- [ ] Physical verification of assets done at least once a year
- [ ] Fully depreciated assets still in use — remove from active list or write off
- [ ] Capital expenditure not charged to revenue (and vice versa)
- [ ] IT Act depreciation computed correctly (WDV method)
- [ ] Companies Act depreciation computed correctly (SLM/WDV as per Schedule II)
- [ ] Deferred tax computed on timing differences between IT and Companies Act depreciation

### Investments
- [ ] All investments valued at cost or market value (whichever lower) — AS-13
- [ ] Investment in subsidiary companies disclosed separately
- [ ] Income from investments properly credited to P&L

### Debtors / Receivables
- [ ] Ageing analysis prepared: 0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 91-180, >180 days
- [ ] Debts outstanding > 6 months — specific provision created?
- [ ] Debtors with credit balance identified (overpayment by debtor)
- [ ] Bad debts written off with management approval
- [ ] Related party debtors disclosed as per AS-18
- [ ] TDS receivable reconciled with Form 26AS

### Loans and Advances
- [ ] Secured vs unsecured loans separately disclosed
- [ ] Loans to directors/related parties disclosed
- [ ] Advance to suppliers recovered or provision made if doubtful
- [ ] Staff advances recovered from salary

### Cash and Bank
- [ ] Cash balance physically verified (cash count done)
- [ ] Bank reconciliation statement prepared for each bank account
- [ ] All bank accounts disclosed in schedules
- [ ] Cheques issued but not presented — included in bank balance
- [ ] No excess cash holding (red flag for cash-intensive business)

### Stock / Inventory
- [ ] Physical stock count done at year end
- [ ] Valuation method: cost or NRV, whichever lower (AS-2)
- [ ] FIFO or Weighted Average — method disclosed and consistently followed
- [ ] Damaged/obsolete stock — provision made or written off
- [ ] Consignment stock excluded from books

### Creditors / Payables
- [ ] Creditors with debit balance identified (overpayment to creditor)
- [ ] Old creditors outstanding > 2 years — verification required (income or genuine?)
- [ ] Related party creditors disclosed
- [ ] Provision for expenses made (electricity, rent, professional fees unpaid at year end)
- [ ] TDS payable reconciled with TDS returns

### Loans (Liability)
- [ ] Loan agreements obtained and filed
- [ ] Interest rate and repayment schedule verified
- [ ] Loans from directors: Section 73 of Companies Act compliance
- [ ] Loans in cash > ₹20,000 — violation of Section 269SS?
- [ ] Long-term vs short-term portion correctly classified

## P&L Audit Checklist

### Sales / Revenue
- [ ] All sales invoices accounted for
- [ ] Revenue recognition policy followed (AS-9)
- [ ] Sales returns properly accounted
- [ ] Credit notes issued for returns — matched with original invoices
- [ ] Advance received for future delivery — not recognized as revenue yet
- [ ] GST collected and reconciled with GSTR-1

### Purchases
- [ ] All purchase invoices accounted for
- [ ] Purchase returns properly accounted
- [ ] Year-end purchases — goods received vs invoiced date checked
- [ ] Direct vs indirect expenses — correct classification
- [ ] GST input credit reconciled with GSTR-2B

### Expenses — Common Checks
- [ ] Salary: TDS deducted, PF/ESI deposited, PT deducted and deposited
- [ ] Rent: TDS deducted @ 10% if > ₹2.4 lakh p.a.
- [ ] Professional fees: TDS @ 10% if > ₹30,000
- [ ] Contractor payments: TDS @ 1%/2% if > ₹30,000 / ₹1 lakh aggregate
- [ ] Cash payments > ₹10,000 identified (Section 40A(3) disallowance)
- [ ] Personal expenses in P&L identified (add back in tax computation)
- [ ] Prior period expenses separately disclosed (AS-5)

### Depreciation
- [ ] Correct rates applied per IT Act schedule
- [ ] New assets added during year — proportionate depreciation
- [ ] Assets sold during year — gain/loss on sale calculated correctly
- [ ] WDV as per books matches IT depreciation working

## TDS Audit Checklist

- [ ] All payments above threshold — TDS deducted at correct rate
- [ ] TDS deposited by 7th of next month (30 April for March)
- [ ] TDS returns filed on time (quarterly)
- [ ] Form 16/16A issued to deductees on time
- [ ] TDS amount in books matches Form 26AS
- [ ] Late deposit interest calculated and paid (1.5% per month)
- [ ] Section 40(a)(ia) disallowances identified — where TDS not deducted

## GST Audit Checklist (Summary)

- [ ] GSTR-1 output matches books
- [ ] GSTR-3B tax paid matches liability in books
- [ ] ITC in books matches GSTR-2B
- [ ] ITC on blocked items not claimed
- [ ] 180-day rule compliance checked
- [ ] RCM liability identified and paid
- [ ] Annual return GSTR-9 filed if applicable
