## What It Is

VirtualCA is an **AI-powered SaaS platform** for Indian SMBs and CA firms that use Tally for accounting.

Most businesses using Tally:
- Make wrong ledger grouping errors they never catch
- Miss TDS deposit deadlines (7th every month)
- Forget PT deposits via Grips (21st every month)
- Have unreconciled bank entries sitting for months
- Get hit with penalties they could have avoided

**VirtualCA solves all of this** by:
1. Reading your Tally export file (Excel / XML / Ledger / Bank / GST JSON)
2. Scanning every ledger against 20+ accounting rules
3. Flagging every error in plain English with the exact fix
4. Giving step-by-step Tally navigation to correct it
5. Letting you export corrections back into Tally
