## Who Is VirtualCA For?

| User | Their Pain | How We Help |
|---|---|---|
| **SMB Owner / Director** | Doesn't know if books are correct | Instant full audit report in 30 seconds |
| **In-house Accountant** | Makes ledger grouping errors | Error list with exact Tally fix steps |
| **CA Firm** | Reviews 10–20 clients manually every month | Multi-client dashboard + team assign workflow |
| **Startup Finance Team** | Misses compliance deadlines | Calendar with live overdue alerts |
| **Business Owner (Kolkata)** | PT deductions & Grips deposit always missed | Auto PT slab + employee-wise + Grips guide |

---

## Primary Persona — SMB Owner

- **Company size:** 5–100 employees
- **Revenue:** ₹50L – ₹50Cr
- **Accounting:** Tally ERP 9 or TallyPrime
- **Pain:** Pays CA ₹15K–₹40K/year for audit that finds errors that could have been caught earlier
- **Goal:** Clean books, no penalties, peace of mind

## Secondary Persona — CA Firm

- **Size:** 1–10 CAs + staff
- **Clients:** 10–50 companies on Tally
- **Pain:** Reviews each client manually — time consuming, error-prone
- **Goal:** Handle more clients with same team, look more professional

## Tertiary Persona — In-house Accountant

- **Works at:** SMB or startup
- **Tool:** Tally daily
- **Pain:** Doesn't always know if grouping is correct, misses deadlines
- **Goal:** Reduce errors, avoid blame, stay compliant

---

## Geography

**Primary:** Kolkata, West Bengal (PT focus)
**Secondary:** All of India (TDS, GST, Tally)
